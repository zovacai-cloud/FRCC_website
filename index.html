Who We Are

Food Regulation & Compliance Centre

Food Regulation and Compliance Centre (FRCC) is an independent, research and advocacy based multi-disciplinary national level scientific organization providing strength to national food regulatory frameworks and enhancing its compliance through independent scientific advice.

FRCC also educates consumers regarding food safety compliance and effective implementation of Food Safety Management System. Our scientists and regulatory experts will help you navigate requirements every step of the way — from product development to market launch.

Regulatory Compliance Services

Food Safety Compliance

Food Safety & FSSAI Compliance

It is the statutory obligation to treat food intended for human consumption in a controlled and managed way. Our Experts help to maintain all food safety compliance requirements from licensing to product approvals, labeling guidance, and formulation development.

  • Food Safety Management System (FSMS) Development & Implementation
  • FSSAI License – State & Central (all categories)
  • FSSAI Registration Certificate
  • Registration for Shop & Establishment Act, Legal Metrology, MSME
  • Modification/Renewal of License & Annual Return
  • Shelf Life Study & Product Approval
  • Advising on Food Labeling and Advertising
  • Formulation Designing & Development
  • Process Development and Optimization
  • Organic Certification
  • Food Product Testing / Compliance Report
  • New Food Manufacturing Unit & Restaurant Setup
Environment Compliance

Environmental Compliance Services

Environmental compliance specialists ensure that companies follow environmental laws and regulations, protect profits, and limit exposure to penalties.

  • Environment and Health Safety Management System
  • Central Pollution Control Board / State Pollution Control Board Compliance
  • Consent to Establishment (CTE)
  • Consent to Operate (CTO)
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Environmental Clearance for Roads / Builder / Malls
  • Air Quality Management
  • Noise Management
  • Waste Handling & Disposal
Water & Waste Water

Water & Wastewater Compliance

We have extensive regulation expertise regarding water and offer comprehensive solutions for water quality management and compliance.

  • Water RO / Softener Installation
  • ETP / STP Installation
  • Water Testing as per FSSAI Norms
  • Central Ground Water Board Certificate / NOC
  • Implementation of Zero Discharge Policy
  • Water Conservation & Ground Recharge
Training Programs

Food Safety Training & Certification

FRCC is an active foSTaC training partner with FSSAI. We offer all foSTaC courses on-site as per industry requirement.

  • FSSAI foSTaC Training
  • Food Safety and Management System (FSMS)
  • Organic Certification Training
  • Food Startup Training for Entrepreneurs
  • Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP Training)
  • Good Hygiene Practices (GHP Training)
  • Good Lab Practices (GLP Training)
  • HACCP (All Levels)
  • Internal Auditor Training for ISO / FSSC / OHSAS
  • Fire Safety and Emergency Preparedness Training
  • Product Recall / Allergen Handling / High Risk Material Handling
Audit Services

Food Safety & Quality Audits

Food safety and quality audits are used for evaluating management systems, certifications, assessing premises and products, and confirming legal compliance.

  • Internal / External – Third Party Audit
  • Food Safety / Compliance Audit
  • Inspection or Vendor Audit
  • Risk Assessment Audit
  • Third Party Manufacturing Audit
  • Root Cause Analysis and Corrective Action
  • HACCP / FSMS Audit
  • Fire Safety and Emergency Preparedness Audit
Export / Import

Export & Import Compliance

We fulfill your basic import/export requirements so you can focus on growing your business.

  • FSSAI Import / Export License
  • FSSAI Import Clearance
  • IEC – Import Export Code
  • Export Council of India Compliance
  • APEDA Compliance
  • Quality Assessment of Exported Food
  • EU / USA and Gulf Country Export Compliance
Food Testing

Food Testing & Laboratory Services

Food testing and analysis is an essential part of the food safety ecosystem to assure that the food is safe to consume as per legal food standard.

  • In-House Lab Setting
  • Third Party Product Testing
  • Lab Accreditation: NABL / BIS / FSSAI / APEDA
  • Lab Facility Expansion with Help of Government Schemes
