Who We Are

Food Regulation & Compliance Centre

Food Regulation and Compliance Centre (FRCC) is an independent, research and advocacy based multi-disciplinary national level scientific organization providing strength to national food regulatory frameworks and enhancing its compliance through independent scientific advice.

Established in 2015, FRCC also educates consumers regarding food safety compliance and effective implementation of Food Safety Management System. Our scientists and regulatory experts will help you navigate requirements every step of the way — from product development to market launch.

FSSAI Licensing

FSSAI License & Registration Services

FSSAI License is a mandatory requirement for all food businesses operating in India. FRCC provides end-to-end FSSAI licensing support — from new applications to renewals, modifications, and annual compliance. Our experts ensure your business is fully compliant at every stage.

All food businesses — manufacturers, traders, importers, restaurants, and e-commerce food sellers — require a valid FSSAI License or Registration to operate legally in India.

  • FSSAI License – State License (Annual Turnover ₹12L–₹20Cr)
  • FSSAI License – Central License (Annual Turnover above ₹20Cr / importers / multi-state)
  • FSSAI Registration Certificate (Annual Turnover up to ₹12 Lakhs)
  • Registration for Shop & Establishment Act
  • Legal Metrology Registration
  • Startup & MSME Registration
  • Modification of FSSAI License
  • Renewal of License & Annual Return Filing
  • Shelf Life Study & Product Approval
  • Organic Certification
  • Calibration & Equipment Compliance
  • FSSAI Product Approval & NOC

FSSAI License & Registration Services

It is a statutory obligation to handle food intended for human consumption in a controlled and managed manner. The key requirements of the Act are that food must comply with food safety standards, must be "of the nature, substance and quality demanded", and must be correctly described and labelled. Our experts help maintain the following compliances:

  • Food Safety Management System Development & Implementation
  • FSSAI License — State & Central License (Importer, Manufacturer, Marketer, Distributor, Storage, Transporter, Re-Packer, Relabeller and Exporter, etc.)
  • FSSAI Registration Certificate
  • Registration for — The Shop and Establishment Act, Legal Metrology Act, Start-Up India, Make in India and MSME
  • Modification / Renewal in License
  • Annual Return
  • Shelf Life Study
  • Product Approval
  • Advising on Food Labelling and Advertising
  • Formulation Designing & Development
  • Process Development and Optimization
  • Organic Certification
  • Calibration & Validation of Equipment
