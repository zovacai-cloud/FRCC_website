Who We Are

Food Regulation & Compliance Centre

Food Regulation and Compliance Centre (FRCC) is an independent, research and advocacy based multi-disciplinary national level scientific organization providing strength to national food regulatory frameworks and enhancing its compliance through independent scientific advice.

FRCC also educates consumers regarding food safety compliance and effective implementation of Food Safety Management System. FRCC navigates the scientific and regulatory requirements that products must meet in order to be marketed to the public.

Our scientists and regulatory experts will help you navigate the scientific and regulatory requirements every step of the way — from product development to market launch.

Food Safety Compliance

Food Safety & FSSAI Compliance

It is the statutory obligation to treat food intended for human consumption in a controlled and managed way. The key requirements of the Act are that food must comply with food safety requirements, must be "of the nature, substance and quality demanded", and must be correctly described (labelled).

Our Experts help to maintain all food safety compliance requirements from licensing to product approvals, labeling guidance, and formulation development.

  • Food Safety Management System (FSMS) Development & Implementation
  • FSSAI License – State & Central (Importer, Manufacturer, Marketer, Distributor, Storage, Transporter, Re-Packer, Re-labeler, Exporter)
  • FSSAI Registration Certificate
  • Registration for Shop & Establishment Act, Legal Metrology Act, Start-Up, Make in India, MSME
  • Modification/Renewal of License & Annual Return
  • Shelf Life Study & Product Approval
  • Advising on Food Labeling and Advertising
  • Formulation Designing & Development
  • Process Development and Optimization
  • Organic Certification
  • Calibration & Validation of Equipment
  • Food Product Testing / Compliance Report
  • New Food Manufacturing Unit & Restaurant Setup
Environment Compliance

Environmental Compliance Services

Complying with environmental rules and regulations isn't just a matter of feeling better about your company's carbon footprint — it's absolutely essential to protecting profits and limiting your company's exposure to litigation and penalties.

Environmental compliance specialists ensure that companies and other agencies follow environmental laws and regulations. They may study the environmental impact of a company's protocols and suggest improvements.

  • Environment and Health Safety Management System
  • Central Pollution Control Board / State Pollution Control Board Compliance
  • Consent to Establishment (CTE)
  • Consent to Operate (CTO)
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Environmental Clearance for Roads / Builder / Malls
  • Air Quality Management
  • Noise Management
  • Waste Handling & Disposal
Water & Waste Water

Water & Wastewater Compliance

The food industry needs large amounts of water as an ingredient of their products, but also — in large amounts — for the production process and for the cleaning of raw materials, machines, and equipment. Water quality matters a lot for your product quality.

We have extensive regulation expertise regarding water and offer comprehensive solutions for water quality management and compliance.

  • Water RO / Softener Installation
  • ETP / STP Installation
  • Water Testing as per FSSAI Norms
  • Central Ground Water Board Certificate / NOC
  • Implementation of Zero Discharge Policy
  • Water Conservation & Ground Recharge

Audit Services

Food Safety & Quality Audits

Food safety and quality audits are used widely in the food industry for various reasons such as to evaluate management systems, obtain certifications to certain food safety and quality standards, assess the condition of premises and products, and confirm legal compliance.

The audit procedure usually incorporates documentation review and conduction of checks and interviews to confirm compliance with a standard. The auditing process may involve the inspection of the whole manufacturing process from raw material through preparation to dispatch.

  • Internal / External – Third Party Audit
  • Food Safety / Compliance Audit
  • Inspection or Vendor Audit
  • Risk Assessment Audit
  • Third Party Manufacturing Audit
  • Root Cause Analysis and Corrective Action
  • HACCP / FSMS Audit
  • Fire Safety and Emergency Preparedness Audit
Export / Import

Export & Import Compliance

Every product requires custom clearance when it goes from one state to another and one country to another. Import and export of goods into and outside a country should undergo a customs clearance process. The importer and exporter of goods should submit valid documents to clear this process.

To start your export-import business, we fulfill your basic requirements so that you can focus on other aspects of growing your business.

  • FSSAI Import / Export License
  • FSSAI Import Clearance
  • IEC – Import Export Code (required for import/export business)
  • Export Council of India Compliance
  • APEDA – Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority Compliance
  • Quality Assessment of Exported Food
  • EU / USA and Gulf Country Export Compliance
Food Testing

Food Testing & Laboratory Services

Food Testing and analysis is an essential part of the food safety ecosystem to assure that the food is safe to consume or as per legal food standard. The food testing industry works closely with manufacturers to ensure that all products are scientifically tested and proved to be safe for human consumption at a variety of stages.

Food testing can cover many aspects of the food manufacturing process — from raw material to production line to finished product. Most commonly, a common set of scientific techniques are used to analyze, assess, and record the safety and viability of a product.

  • In-House Lab Setting
  • Third Party Product Testing
  • Lab Accreditation: NABL / BIS / FSSAI / APEDA
  • Lab Facility Expansion with Help of Government Schemes
